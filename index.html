About Bad Dogs NFT

‍

What is it?

This is a personal project by Keegan Leary to see if he could make an NFT minting dapp in Webflow.

‍

How much does it cost?

1 NFT costs 1 MATIC token (+gas) on the Polygon network. As of this moment (3JAN2022) 1 MATIC = $2.50 USD.

‍

How many can I mint at once?

10 is the max mint number.

‍

What does it do?

Not much other than exist on the blockchain :).

‍

How do I get MATIC?

Check out this YouTube video.

‍

Where do I see it after I mint one?

It will show up in your Opensea.io account automatically.
